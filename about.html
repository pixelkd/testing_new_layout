About Me

I’ve been creating stories since I was a kid, sketching characters and scenes from the world around me. One of my earliest achievements was getting a comic strip published in a local newspaper as a teenager—a moment that convinced me my passion for storytelling was more than just a hobby. I later earned a degree in Animation from the Art Institute of Houston and have continued learning, experimenting, and refining my craft ever since.

Over time, I've honed my skills working in games, comic books, and storyboards for independent films, where I enjoy transforming concepts into compelling visuals. I value punctuality, clear communication, and a friendly approach to collaboration, ensuring each project runs smoothly. At the heart of my work is a commitment to bringing narratives to life in a way that captivates readers and viewers alike.
